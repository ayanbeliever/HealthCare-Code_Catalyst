About Ruralwell

Ruralwell is an IoT-enabled rural health kiosk initiative that aims to bridge the healthcare gap in remote areas. By leveraging smart medical sensors and telemedicine, we provide affordable and real-time healthcare access to underserved communities. Our mission is to improve healthcare accessibility, promote preventive care, and reduce the burden on hospitals.
